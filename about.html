About Me

Hi there! My name is Millanda Zhou and I'm an aspiring front-end developer.

I’m based in Seattle and am a self-taught web developer with a passion for front-end development. I graduated from the University of Washington with a Bachelor of Arts in Communication Studies. Afterwards, I worked in the accounting and administrative fields for about five years, eventually becoming a Full Charge Bookkeeper in charge of the overall accounting books. I am very detail-oriented and focused on making processes more efficient.

I have always been curious about a career in web development. When I was younger, I loved to customize and build webpages for fun. In 2022, I decided to fully commit to this career change, and I enrolled in the SheCodes bootcamp program. All of the languages and technologies that I have learned so far has been very exciting for me - I’m realizing how much more I can do now compared to back when I was a kid.

I’m passionate about design, and websites that are responsive and user friendly. I’m excited to continue learning as I move forward as a front-end developer and I look forward to any future opportunities to gain that experience. Right now, I am open to front-end developer positions. If you are interested in working with me, feel free to contact me here.
